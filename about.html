About Me

Who is Jayden?

I'm an 18 year old Freshman at Borough of Manhattan Community College. I enjoy baking sweet treats like; cookies, cakes, etc., playing guitar (which I have been for almost a year), and playing video games. I've been using Adobe software like Illustrator and Photoshop for more than 3 years. I strive to become a succesful UX Designer/Graphic Designer to help those who cannot bring their brilliant ideas to life.
